Privacy Policy

At Home Automation Inc., we take your privacy and data security seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Data Security

We employ state-of-the-art security measures to protect your data. This includes:

  • Utilizing blockchain technology to ensure the integrity of transactions and data.
  • Implementing SHA-256 encryption to securely hash sensitive information.
  • Using AES encryption to encrypt data at rest and in transit.
  • Encrypting cookies to prevent unauthorized access to session information.

Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information for the following purposes:

  • Account registration: We collect basic information such as name, email address, and contact details to create and manage user accounts.
  • Usage data: We collect information on how you interact with our services to improve user experience and troubleshoot issues.

Usage of Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized content. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Securely manage login sessions and authenticate users.
  • Analyze usage patterns and optimize website performance.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is strictly used for:

  • Providing and improving our services.
  • Communicating with you regarding updates, promotions, and support.
